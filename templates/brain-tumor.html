Our brain tumor detection system uses a 3D U-Net architecture combined with a classification network, trained on over 10,000 annotated MRI volumes.

  • Multi-sequence analysis (T1, T2, FLAIR, etc.)

  • Regular updates with new training data

Tumor Types We Detect

Glioma

Including astrocytoma, oligodendroglioma, and glioblastoma

Meningioma

Slow-growing tumors that form on the meninges

Pituitary Adenoma

Tumors that form in the pituitary gland

Acoustic Neuroma

Benign tumors that develop on the vestibular nerve

Metastatic Tumors

Cancer that has spread from elsewhere in the body

Medulloblastoma

Fast-growing tumors that form at the base of the skull

Craniopharyngioma

Rare tumors near the pituitary gland

Ependymoma

Tumors that form in the ependyma cells
