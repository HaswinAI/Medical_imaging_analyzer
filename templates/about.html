About AI Radiology

We're on a mission to transform healthcare through artificial intelligence and advanced medical imaging analysis.

Our Story

AI Radiology was founded in 2024 by a team of radiologists, AI researchers, and healthcare technology experts with a shared vision: to harness the power of artificial intelligence to improve medical imaging diagnosis.

What began as a research project at a leading medical institution has grown into a comprehensive AI platform that serves healthcare providers worldwide. Our team has expanded to include experts in machine learning, software engineering, regulatory affairs, and clinical implementation.

Today, AI Radiology's solutions are used in hundreds of hospitals and imaging centers across 30 countries, helping radiologists detect diseases earlier and with greater accuracy than ever before.
